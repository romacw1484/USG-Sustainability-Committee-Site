Fordham Flea

What We Do

Fordham Flea is a student-run campus-wide thrift store. We are committed to promoting second-hand shopping and sustainable fashion practices. We take student donations and host flea market events around campus for students to shop. All proceeds go toward supporting sustainability initiatives on campus.
