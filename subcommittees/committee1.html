Committee 1

What We Do

Committee 1 focuses on improving campus sustainability practices. We work closely with facilities to implement eco-friendly solutions in dorms and cafeterias, reduce waste, and educate students about greener living.

Join us on Tuesdays at 5 PM in the Student Center to share ideas, volunteer for upcoming projects, and help make our campus a model of sustainability.
