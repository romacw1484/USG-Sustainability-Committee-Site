Events and Communications

What We Do

Our events team plans and hosts programming such as lectures, sustainable crafts, and community outreach. Each semester, we have week-long events for Earth Week & Sustainability Week, which draw large student participation. Our team practices project management as we budget, schedule, and facilitate each event ourselves.
In tandem, our communications team manages our social media presence and the marketing for our programming and operations. Our Instagram platform is a space to keep our following updated on Suscom events and Fordham-related sustainability news. The team uses design software to create posters & posts to spread the word about our work.

Join us on Tuesdays at 5 PM in the Student Center to share ideas, volunteer for upcoming projects, and help make our campus a model of sustainability.
