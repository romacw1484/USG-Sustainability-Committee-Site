Eco Representatives

What We Do

Our Eco-Representatives subcommittee members serve as peer educators on campus. Through initiatives like informational tabling, student lectures, and infographics, we teach our community how to live more sustainable lives, as well as inform them about environmental issues and the urgency of combatting them. We also collaborate with Events & Communications to organize Earth Week events that engage students in eco-friendly activities, promoting the creation of long-lasting sustainable habits.
